About

System Analyst since 2004 with Data Processing Technology background. Over a decade juggling with frontend and backend. To spice things up, I'm now also working with iOS mobile applications.

Some of my background includes 3 years at CUBOCC, one of the largest advertising agencies in Brazil. I currently work at Globosat, the largest television cable service in Latin America.

So, what am I doing here? Simply put, besides my passion for coding and technology, I love training and supervising teams, as well as getting my hands dirty from day one, taking part in the early stages of a project's concept and helping to define features and solutions to a product.

That being said, feel free to contact me whether you're a team member, product owner or stakeholder - I'll be thrilled to join you in a new adventure.
